Raw Vegan Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

For the layer:

2 cups soaked almonds (overnight)

1 1/2 cups soaked walnuts (for at least 4 hours

6 Tbs. Maple syrup (I used Class A)

10 pitted dates

1/2 cup raw cacao

For the frosting:

2 avocados

4 Tbs. raw cacao

1/2 cup Maple syrup

6 pitted dates

Steps

1. Grind the almonds, walnuts and cacao in a food processor

2. Add the maple syrup and the pitted dates and mix again until you achieve a smooth sticky texture. Press the mixture into a glass baking dish or just form a circle on a plate with half the mixture. The Maple syrup will hold the moist ingredients together. With the rest of the mixture make the second layer.

3. Combine the avocados, raw cacao, maple syrup and pitted dates in a food processor/blender. This will be your frosting.

4. Frost one of the halves, then put the other layer of the cake on top. Finally, frost the top of the double-layered cake with the remainder of the frosting.

5. Add some nuts or coconut on top and slice into 8 medium pieces (or 12 small pieces).
