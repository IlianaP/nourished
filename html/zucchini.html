Raw Zucchini Pasta with Pesto Sauce

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

Large zucchini (1 per person)

Lemon juice

For the Vegan Pesto Sauce:

1 large bunch of basil

1/3 cup walnuts (or other nuts: pine nuts etc.)

1 large clove garlic

Salt to taste

6 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil

1/4 cup (or more) nutritional yeast (if you want this cheesy taste)

1 small tomato

Steps

1. Prepare your zucchini noodles julienne style or spiralized with a fancy tool like this or just use a humble box grater. If you prefer cooked and warm food, you could prepare for example your favorite whole grain pasta of choice.

2. Place all ingredients into a food processor. Process to a finely ground consistency and smooth and creamy. Don't put the sauce directly on top if you have cooked your pasta, because you don't want all your vitamins to disappear.

3. Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
