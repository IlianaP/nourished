Sprouted Buckwheat Granola

Ingredients

3 cups raw buckwheat

Maple syrup or honey or raw coconut nectar to taste

Fresh lemon juice (optional)

1/3 tablespoon or more cinnamon

Salt to taste (optional)

Steps

1. Soak the buckwheat overnight in a large bowl and cover with something. Remember that the buckwheat will absorb the water, so add plenty of water.

2. 12 hours later, rinse the buckwheat well.

3. Take a strainer and fill it. Place it over a bowl, but make sure the water doesn't touch the buckwheat. Cover with something.

4.You must keep the buckwheat moist, so every 3-6 hours rinse the buckwheat, depending on your the humidity of your home).

5.Continue doing it for at least 24 hours.

6.After that, rinse the buckwheat and put into a large bowl. Add the other ingredients and mix well.

7.Spread the buckwheat on a baking sheet and "bake them" on the lowest temperature for at least 5 hours or until crispy. I baked mine at 30˚C-40˚C for 8 hours.

8.Store in the fridge and enjoy them with some kind of milk or plain.
