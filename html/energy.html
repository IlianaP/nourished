Raw Energy Bars

Ingredients

Walnuts

Chia seeds

Honey/dates

Honey/dates

Hemp protein

Oats

Sunflower seeds

+other nuts

Some coconut oil

Steps

1. Throw the ingredients in your food processor, then add the dates and process it until everything starts to stick together. If you don't have a food processor, take a bowl and mix all the ingredients in it with your hands. Yum!

2. If the nuts won't stick together, add more honey/dates.

3. Press into a lined pan and set in the fridge for an hour or more.

4. Cut into bars.
