Adzuki Beans

Ingredients

Adzuki Beans (500g)

Water

Onion

Salt to taste

Some vegetable oil (optional)

Red pepper

Steps

1. Soak your beans overnight in a large pot covered with water.

2. After they have soaked, drain them and rinse several times.Place rinsed beans in a large pot on the stove. Pour water over the beans to cover by 2-3 inches. Bring to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer.

3. Skim any foam that might rise to the top while cooking, and add additional water (or stock), if there does not seem to be enough liquid.

4. Cook until the beans are tender, about 1 1/2 hours.

5. Add some salt, Onion, vegetable oil and red pepper.

6. Serve hot.
