Vegan Chocolate Ice Cream

Ingredients

One coconut (the flesh)

2 bananas

Maple syrup/ honey/ stevia to taste

Fruits (optional)

Cacao Powder to taste

Steps

1. Open the coconut and take out the flesh.

2. With a juicer, make your full-fat milk.

3. Blend the milk, bananas, cacao and fruits together.


If you have an ice cream machine:

1. Place the mixture into the bowl and make your ice cream with your ice cream machine.

2. Serve cold with fruits.


If you don't have an ice cream machine:

1. Place the mixture in the freezer and mix well every 30 minutes until desired consistency.

2. Serve cold with fruits.
