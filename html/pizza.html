Flourless Vegan Pizza (Cauliflower dough)

Ingredients

For the dough:

One medium cauliflower head

4 tablespoons flax seeds

12 tablespoons water

Salt to taste

Toppings:

Corn

Cashew cheese or Daiya cheese or other vegan cheese

Onions

Homemade Tomato Sauce (like the one from here)

Olives etc.

Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350 ºF/180ºC.

2. Chop all the cauliflower florets into small pieces, place in a blender and blend until pureed. It must look like rice.

3. Place the pureed cauliflower mixture into a cheesecloth over a large bowl (to catch the liquid) and squeeze the excess water from the cauliflower-> puree.

4.Combine the remaining ingredients (flax seeds and water) into the large bowl and hand mix with a large spoon.

5. Spread the dough into either 1 large pizza shape or 2 small pizza rounds on a cooking sheet.

6.Don't make it too thin.

7.Bake at 350 ºF/180ºC for about 20-25 minutes, until the top is golden brown.

8.Take out of the oven and top with your favorite toppings.

9.Bake for another 10 minutes.
