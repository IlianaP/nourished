Cherry Muffins

Ingredients

1 and 1/2 cup Whole wheat flour

3/4 cups brown sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 spoon baking soda

2 spoons apple cider vinegar

1/3 cup coconut oil (or any other vegetable oil)

1 large banana, mashed (about 1/2 cup)

1/3 cup coconut milk (or any other non-dairy milk)

1 cup fresh cherries

Steps

1. Preheat oven to 190℃/400℉. Prepare the muffin cups.

2. Combine flour, sugar and salt. Combine baking soda and apple cider vinegar and set aside until the bubbles are gone. Then add to the flour, sugar and salt.

3. Add in milk of choice, banana, and oil and stir until just moistened. Fold in cherries. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full if you want them to be big, if not, then only 1/2 full.

4. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
