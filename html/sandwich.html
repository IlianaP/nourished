Vegan Ice Cream Sandwich

Ingredients

For the cookies:

1/2 cup coconut oil

1 cup whole wheat flour

3 teaspoons honey (if not, substitute for 1/2 brown sugar or something else)

1 1/2 Tbsp freshly ground flax seeds

1/2 tablespoon baking soda

1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Salt to taste

2 tablespoons water or vegan milk

+1/2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

For the banana ice cream:

3 normal bananas (frozen)

Cacao (optional)

Other ingredients like coconut etc. (optional)

Steps

1. Put the bananas into the freezer until really hard.

2. Preheat the oven to 200℃/400℉.

3. Meanwhile, prepare the cookies: Combine the flour, salt.

4. Grind your flax seeds in a seed grinder or coffee grinder. Add them to the dry bowl.

5. In another small bowl combine the baking soda and the 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (bubbles :D). Wait until the bubbles are away and add to the flour. Mix well.

6. In another bowl add the coconut oil and honey and water. Mix well and add in the main bowl. (Make sure all ingredients have a room temperature)

7. Stir well until you have a wet thick yet oily dough. Fold in the vinegar.

8. Put it in the refrigerator until it becomes sticky and easy to form (about 10 minutes)

9. Make your own cookies and place them in the oven.

10. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown. The cookies will be very soft when you take them out. Wait a little bit before you eat them.

The ice cream:

1. Take the bananas out of the freezer and put them into a food processor. Blend until ice cream forms and add any ingredients you want: coconut, sugar, cacao etc.

2. Place the ice cream on a baking sheet and make a thin rectangle out of it. Place in the freezer until hard.

3. After the ice cream is ready, take it out and cut exactly the same shapes.

4. Use 2 cookies and one ice cream shape to make the sandwiches and put them back into the freezer for 10 mintes, after which you can serve them.

Enjoy!
