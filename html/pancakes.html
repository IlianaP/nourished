Crepes (Dairy-free/Egg-free)

Ingredients

1 cup whole-wheat flour

1/2 cup coconut milk or any other vegan milk, plus more to thin

4 tablespoons coconut oil or any other vegetable oil

2/3 cup water

Salt (optional)

Sugar (optional)

Vanilla (optional)

Steps

1. In a large bowladd all ingredients to form a thin batter.

2. Add a bit more milk to achieve a thin consistency, if necessary.

3. You could refrigerate them for 30 minutes, but this is not necessary.

4. Place a crepe nonstick pan over medium-high heat.

5. Pour just enough batter in the pan to cover the bottom, swirling to evenly distribute. Cook for couple of minutes or just until batter sets. Flip your crepe and cook until both sides are lightly browned. Repeat with remaining batter.

6. Add your favorite filling: honey, agave, homemade strawberry jelly, fruits etc.
