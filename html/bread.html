Gluten-Free Vegan Bread

Ingredients

2/3 almond milk (or any other non-dairy milk)

1 cup unroasted cashews

2/3 cup certified gluten-free oat flour

1/4 cup ground flax seeds

1/4 cup amaranth flour

1 Tbs. Apple cider vinegar

1/2 Tbs. Baking Soda

1/3 cup seeds (I used sunflower seeds)

1 tsp Himalayan salt

Steps

1. Preheat the oven (200ªC/400ºF).

2. Grind the cashews, but don't do this for too long, because cashews become sticky.

3. In a large mixing bowl add all dry ingredients.

4. Add the almond milk and mix well. If the mixture is too dry, add some more milk.

5. Take a small loaf pan and place a baking sheet over it. I prefer not to use a loaf pan- I just form it into a particular shape and put the dough on a baking sheet. Sprinkle on top some seeds, whatever kind you prefer.

6. Bake in the preheated for 1 1/2 – 2 hours or until the crust of the bread becomes golden brown.

7. Set it aside for at least 15 minutes before cutting it into slices.
