Almond Butter Salad

Ingredients

2 big organic peaches

1 cup blueberries

2 cups raspberries

2 cups cherries

2-3 tablespoons almond butter

Steps

1. Wash all fruits and put the raspberries and blueberries into a bowl.

2. Chop the peaches into large pieces.

3. Remove the pits from the cherries using a knife and cutting them into two.

4. Put the cherries into the bowl and add 2 or three tablespoons almond butter.

5. You can mix them if you want.
